About WorkBuddya

WorkBuddya is a modern task marketplace platform designed to connect people and businesses across Sri Lanka with skilled and reliable manpower. Our mission is simple — to make it easier, faster, and safer to get tasks completed.

Our Mission

From household services and repairs to business support and professional services, WorkBuddya provides a trusted space where tasks can be posted, offers can be received, and work can be completed with confidence.

We believe in transparency, reliability, and empowering both clients and service providers through technology.

Our Vision

"To become Sri Lanka’s most trusted digital platform for connecting skilled manpower with people and businesses who need work done efficiently and professionally."
